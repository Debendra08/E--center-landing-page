Our Models

Shadab E-Rickshaw

A reliable and eco-friendly electric rickshaw designed for smooth passenger transport. It offers excellent mileage, low maintenance, and strong build quality, making it perfect for daily use. Ideal for affordable and sustainable transportation

Shadab Loader E-Rickshaw

A durable and eco-friendly electric rickshaw designed for heavy-duty goods transport. Built with a strong frame and powerful motor, it delivers smooth performance and excellent mileage. With low maintenance and running costs, it’s perfect for businesses, cargo delivery, and logistics, offering a reliable and sustainable transportation solution.

Fantacy Pro scooter

The Fantacy Pro is a lightweight electric scooter (65 KG) made in India. It can go up to 25 KM/H and travel 80–100 KM on a single charge. It comes with a 250W motor, LED headlight, alloy wheels, remote anti-theft, and waterproof throttle. Stylish, safe, and perfect for city rides!

Kaisai electric bicycle

Kaisai is a stylish and powerful electric bicycle with a 350W motor, long-lasting battery, fat tires, and dual suspension. It features pedal assist, bright lights, a digital display, security locks, and convenient storage for a smooth and practical ride.

Kaisai Electric Mopeds

Kaisai electric mopeds are eco-friendly and stylish, powered by lithium batteries that give 80–90 KM range and speeds of 45–50 KM/H. Made by Wuxi Kaisai Electric Technology Co., Ltd., now these scooters are available in Nepal. Some models feature 20-inch wheels and extra safety features for a smooth, secure ride.

Electric Rickshaw & Home Inverter Batteries

We offer lithium dry batteries for electric rickshaws and acid batteries for rickshaws and home inverters. Brands include Eastman, Duratex, Trontek, Lebo, N-Batt, Cygna, Missile, and more. Lithium batteries come with 3-year warranty, while acid batteries have 6, 12, or 36 months warranty. Reliable, long-lasting power for all your needs.
